Dice Fishing

Catch fish by throwing dice onto the board. Trade your hard-earned fish for gold in order to buy powerful upgrades from the shop. But don’t get too greedy! If you all fish in the same spot, you’ll destroy the fragile ecosystem and get nothing! Whoever has the most money by the end of round 10 wins!
